RFC 2811

channel names:
have prefixes '&', '#', '+' or '!'
are 50 char limited
are case insensitive
shall have no space, ctrlG or comma

& channels are local
channel masks toggle which servers see the channel
there are channel properties, defined by channel modes
+ channels don't support channel modes
channel creators are automatically channel operators, they can toggle what other cops can do

servers must forbid nickname anonymous, beceause of channel flag 'a' (anonymous channel)
